## Step 4: Understanding Signals

### Signal Grades

- **S Grade (90-100)**: GOD TIER - Take these trades!
- **A Grade (80-89)**: EXCELLENT - High confidence
- **B Grade (70-79)**: GOOD - Solid trades
- **C Grade (60-69)**: OKAY - Proceed with caution
- **D Grade (50-59)**: WEAK - Probably skip
- **F Grade (<50)**: AVOID - Stay out!

### Reading the Scores

Each signal shows 5 component scores:

1. **V1 SMC** (0-25): Smart Money structure
2. **V2 Flow** (0-25): Order flow & sentiment
3. **V3 Auction** (0-20): Volume analysis
4. **V4 Quantum** (0-15): Probability models
5. **V5 Psychology** (0-15): Your mental state

### For Beginners

1. **Start with high scores only** (80+)

3. **Keep a journal** - Learn from every trade
4. **Focus on psychology** - It's 90% of trading
